I

Raison D'être

 

Ballard Pier and the Surrounding Ballard Estate is so intrinsic to The City By The Bay, that it would be impossible to imagine the citadel's existence without this landmark district.

Adjacent to the Admiralty, Port Trust and High Court precincts; it became the cornerstone for the Bay City, to become the trading and financial capital of the East Indies, that fueled further expansion to HongKong, Macau and the rest of South East Asia. It was the strategic, military and logistics hub that led to the development and rapid unification of the Seven Islands. We pay homage to this understated, discreet power centre - of the city, the colonies and thereby the Empire.
